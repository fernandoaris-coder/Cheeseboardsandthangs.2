About Us

Rooted in Braselton, GA — we bring people together around food, flavor, and beautiful presentation.

Our Story

CheeseBoards and Thangs started with a simple idea: make gatherings feel special. From intimate boards to full-size grazing tables, we handle design, sourcing, and on-site styling so your event looks and tastes incredible.

Beyond food, we also curate small-batch olive oils and balsamic vinegars, plus a rotating selection of antiques to elevate your home and table.
